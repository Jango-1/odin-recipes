Plain Muffins

Description

This is my blueberry muffin recipe. They are very tasty to eat.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Blueberries

Steps

  1. Combine the water and flour.
  2. Knead the dough until it's right.
  3. Add into a muffin pan.
  4. Add blueberries.
  5. Bake at 450-degrees fahrenheit for 20 minutes.
